Steam

Steam is a cloud-based gaming library. Steam is a video game digital distribution service by Valve and enjoy major market share. Steam is to gaming what Netflix or Amazon Prime Video are to TV.

Technicals

1. Primary Axis

Monthly Average Gamers = Total Steam Users in a month / Days in that month

2. Secondary Axis

Monthly average users of top 3 games

Top 3 Games are DOTA, Counter Strike: Global Offensive,Player Unknown’s Battleground (PUBG). Vertical bars are represented by the average number of PC gamers who plays these three different games. They are distinguished by different colours.

Events

PUBG dominated all the games out there Mar 2017- Jan 2018

PlayerUnknown’s Battlegrounds (PUBG) is an online multiplayer battle royal game originated from South Korea. PUBG was the top-selling premium game of 2017 having sold 30 million copies worldwide and grossed about $900 million. In Jan-18 maximum number of players were seen playing on steam. The count was 3,236,027.

Downfall of PC PUBG and rise of Mobile PUBG Mar 2018- Aug 2018

The decline in the number of gamers on PC is because of the one major factor which is PUBG MOBILE. On 23 March 2017 PUBG Mobile released which took gaming to next level. PC gamers shifted to mobile. There are presently 500 million users all around the globe and 20-25 million monthly active users in India.

The incredible rise of Fortnite June 2018-Aug 2018

In 2018 Fortnite was the highest earning free to play game of 2018. In June 2018 around 150 million registered Fortnite users were there. Today the count is at least 200 million. People started playing Fortnite more than Pubg. Thus, steam graph declined at that time.

Official availability of steam in china Nov 2018 - Jan 19

Steam was not legal in china a couple of years back but it was still popular with gamers who use unofficial ways to access it. China has put restrictions on internet and has blocked many websites like Google, Facebook Twitch etc. Steam was officially approved by government in 2018 end. Steam now has most of the users from china. Majority of them are playing DOTA and PUBG.

Steam record break. More than 20 million people logged in Steam to play games Nov 2019 - Apr 2020

More players than ever. A rare peak was observed on April 20. The COVID-19 is an ongoing global pandemic. The impact of this pandemic is surprisingly vary from industry to industry. While some of them faced major losses some of them boomed. Gaming falls in the latter category. Physical movement around the world was restricted. Due to these restrictions people were left with little options at home. So many turned to gaming.

COVID -19, Government relaxing restrictions Apr 20-Jun 20

Graph is now declining every day as many countries have removed restrictions on travel which were earlier applied due to COVID-19. lockdown rules are relaxed. People are now spending lesser time at home and they are rejoining there work hence the decline.
